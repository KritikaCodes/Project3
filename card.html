Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Legend has it that these adorable herding dogs are not only a favorite of Queen Elizabeth II, but also of fairies and elves of Wales, who used the Pembroke Welsh Corgi to pull fairy coaches, work fairy cattle, and serve as the steed for fairy warriors.

  • Dog Breed Group: Herding Dogs
  • Height: 10 inches to 1 foot tall at shoulder
  • Weight: Up to 30 pounds
  • Life Span: 12 to 14 years

Originally bred to herd cattle, sheep, and horses, the Pembroke Welsh Corgi is an active and intelligent dog breed. Easy to train and eager to learn, Pembrokes are great with children and other pets, and you can find them in four different coat colors and markings.
